Gats.io ESP

A small tampermonkey script that reverse engineers Gats.io, an online multiplayer game. It hooks the network connection to figure out where all players, bullets, and explosives are at any time. Features include:
* Highlighting players, bullets, and explosives, even invisible ones.
* Path and Vector lines drawn between you and other players to help with aim and detection.
* Greenlisting to help determine friend from foe on FFA servers.
* Mousebinds for reloading and special ability for ease of play.
